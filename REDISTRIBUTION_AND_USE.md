# Redistribution and Use Policy

This is a source-available project. GitHub is the technical source and
development record, not the player download channel.

- **Supported downloads:** Get releases, installation instructions, dependency
  information, and player support from the official [itch.io](https://plumantics.itch.io/simstrology-mod),
  [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/sims4/mods/big-three-astrology-mod),
  or [Nexus Mods](https://www.nexusmods.com/thesims4/mods/5219) release page.
- **GitHub archives are not releases:** The **Code > Download ZIP** archive is
  not tested, packaged, or supported for players. Do not place it in your Mods
  folder.
- **Redistribution:** Do not reupload the source, release files, or modified
  builds anywhere without prior written permission.
- **Modification and derivative releases:** Private learning modifications are
  allowed under the license. Public forks, ports, derivative mods, and compiled
  releases require written permission before publication.
- **Translations:** Please ask before publishing a translation, localized build,
  or translated source. Approved translations must credit Plum Antics and link
  to the official release.
- **Monetization:** Do not sell, paywall, bundle for payment, or monetize this
  project or a derivative without prior written permission.
